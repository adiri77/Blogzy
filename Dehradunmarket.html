Tibetan Market

Tibetan Market in Nainital is a vibrant and colorful shopping destination that offers a wide range of merchandise, including clothing, accessories, and handicrafts. ... The market is known for its unique Tibetan-style clothing and jewelry, all available at affordable prices. Visitors can also find a variety of handicrafts, including woolen shawls, carpets, and pottery. The market's bustling atmosphere and friendly vendors make it a delightful place to shop and explore. Additionally, the market's location near Naini Lake offers visitors breathtaking views of the surrounding mountains and scenery. Tibetan Market Nainital is a must-visit destination for anyone looking for a unique shopping experience and a taste of Tibetan culture.

Landour Bazaar

Landour is a quaint little cantonement town in the foothills of the Himalayas at an altitude of about 2200 meters. A twin town of Mussoorie where time is frozen taking you all the way back to the British Raj. ...There are literally two places in Landour where all the economic activities happen. Char Dukaan consisting of a post office, two general stores, a cafe and Sister's Bazaar with its iconic Bakehouse a charming place for its amazing bakery items and A.Prakash & Co. a grocery store which sells some yummy in-house jams, cheese and peanut butter.

Moti Bazaar

This market is between Vishnu Ghat and Har ki Pauri. It remains crowded most of the time. You can buy cheap household items, toys, woolens etc. from here.n most of the shops, prices are fixed ... Moti Bazaar is the main market of Haridwar. It is an attractive place with lots of shops of household articles, handicrafts, blankets and winter garments, stuff that is used in Hindu worship and rituals, brass idols of Hindu deities, toys etc. The place is also famous for Jalebis, Rasgullas, Samosa, Vegetable Pakora, Chole Bhature etc. like mouth watering foods. This is a must visit market while you are travelling to Haridwar. People who are looking for pickles or ayurvedic medicines will find them in abundance here.
